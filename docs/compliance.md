# Compliance

## Canada — CRTC

- Call only between 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM local time
- Identify the business name at the start of every call
- Provide an opt-out mechanism ("Press 9 to be removed")
- Maintain internal DNC list, honored within 14 days
- Do not call numbers on the National DNCL
- Retain call records for 24 months minimum

Reference: https://www.crtc.gc.ca/eng/phone/telemarketing.htm

## United States — TCPA

- Written consent required before auto-dialing cell phones
- Honor DNC requests within 30 days
- Consult legal counsel before launching US campaigns
